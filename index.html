Executive Summary

A nation introduced through sport.

Eagle FC proposes a landmark international MMA and wrestling event beside Gomaspan Dam, anchoring a month-long cultural carnival and broadcasting Erbil to the world. Powered by the global credibility of Khabib Nurmagomedov and the current dominance of Islam Makhachev, this turns a single fight night into lasting tourism, prestige, and economic momentum for Kurdistan.

  • A premium, broadcast-grade live event with a globally recognised promotion
  • A tourism accelerator for a region already welcoming 8M+ visitors a year
  • A private-sector sponsorship platform and a repeatable annual asset

The model is proven.

The distinction. Erbil is not copying Abu Dhabi. Abu Dhabi proved the model; Erbil creates its own identity: authentic, Kurdish, scenic, and premium.

Additional Costs · Production & Operations

Everything around the cage.

These items can be managed and outsourced by KRD Holding, or Eagle FC can advise and fold them into a single budget. Scope depends on what already exists in Erbil versus what is brought in.

1Production Crew

A local production company if one is available, or a full crew brought in from outside.

2Stage & Equipment

Mats, backstage, walkout bridge, lighting, sound, and screens. Sourced locally or shipped in.

3Accommodation & Logistics

Roughly 70 to 90 rooms for 5 days, all international logistics handled by us, and visas.

4Licensing

Sanctioning with the appropriate governing body, where one exists in Kurdistan.

5Medical & Insurance

Lead doctor, fight and backstage doctors, two ambulances on site, and full insurance.

6Marketing

Advertising across radio, TV, internet and social, banners, and branded merchandise.

7Event Staffing

Athletes, judges, referees, cutmen, commentators, announcer, security, and promotional staff.

Flexible

Handled by KRD Holding directly, or advised and coordinated by Eagle FC.

Indicative scope only. Actual numbers are expected to change once local availability and final requirements are confirmed.
